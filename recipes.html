French omelette my way

January 20th, 2020

French omelttes are a relieable dinner for any student with little time to spare. Here is a way to elevate your omelette a touch with cupboard basics and a little care.

Ingredients (serves 1)
  • Eggs: 2
  • Slice of ham: 1
  • Slice of cheese: 1
  • A splash of milk
  • A scoop of butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Basil
  • Oregano
  • Thyme
  • Parsley
Steps
  1. Mix eggs, milk, herbs, salt and pepper in a bowl and whip until slightly foamy and well integrated.
  2. Melt in a pan over high heat and spread over the whole surface.
  3. Add eggy mix from step 1 to the pan and inmediately turn heat down to medium
  4. When the mix has settled, add the slice of cheese and cover width the slice of ham on one half of the omelette.
  5. Try to lift the omelette from the pan with a spatula. When you see that the bottom side is slightly brown, fold along the middle of the omelette onto the half with the ham and the cheese.
  6. Serve with extra parsley and enjoy!
